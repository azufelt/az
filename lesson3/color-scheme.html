Color Theory:

Complimentary Color Scheme

Blue is used at the primary color on the site not only because it is a common representation of weather and weather related events, blue also establishes a sense of trust from the visitors. Blue denotes other appealing qualities such as calmness like the sky and the ocean (both of which reinforce the main element of being a weather-related site.) Incorporating multiple shades of blue and grays give the site a touch of conservatism, keeping the color scheme simple and appealing to the target audience.

The yellow hue chosen is added as an accent color because it creates a harmonious complimentary color scheme with the muted green/blue hue, the yellow also gives the eye a break from overwhelmingly conservative and simple colors. Because the yellow is much brighter than the other colors within the palette, it is used as a call-to-action color. Letting the site visitor know where to navigate to.
