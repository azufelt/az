Site Name:

The Drizzly Bear

The name of this weather site is The Drizzly Bear. The site name is a simple and catchy play on words, which will be entertaining, but respectable to the target audience.

Because of it's weather-centered nature it has drizzly, rain as it's center, incorporates "bear" because that is an animal commonly found in and around the local area.

Purpose:

This site will be a place that brings community members together â€”bridging the gap between community as a whole and the individuals it represents.

Visitors to this site will be able to get local weather updates and weather related alerts such as pending storms, local fire conditions like possible burn bans or emergency alerts. They will also be able to see information regarding upcoming community events and happenings around town, highlights and final scores from the local high School sports teams. Information regardinging area state parks would be able to be accessed as well.

There may even be a local buy & sell page. From time to time special promotions will occur such as town parades, temporary art exhibits and other cultural events.

Domain:

www.thedrizzlybear.com
Available from Go Daddy for $1/month

This domain is easy enough to be remembered, and has no special characters or spellings, which can be confusing to a site visitor. Also, because it is not an official town-sponsored page, it uses a .com in the URL.
